## PRICING MODEL UPDATE: New Weekly Pricing Structure

**Changes:**
- Updated weekly pricing to reflect agreed-upon tiers:
  * Trial: FREE (7 days, up to 3 farms, unlimited users)
  * Startas: €19/week (1-5 farms, unlimited users)
  * Praktika: €39/week (6-15 farms, unlimited users)
  * Augimas: €69/week (16-35 farms, unlimited users)
  * Komanda: €119/week (36+ farms, unlimited users)

- ALL PLANS include all 3 core modules (Veterinarija, Klientai, Vetpraktika)
- ALL PLANS include unlimited users
- Difference is in farm count limits
